A distributed generalized Nash equilibrium (GNE) algorithm achieving state consensus (||x_i|| ~ 0.35 within t < 10 time units) for n=7 healthcare workers, with heterogeneous dual variables reflecting network topology. We formalize clinical worker attributes (skill proficiency, alacrity, workload fairness, communication efficiency) as mathematical quantities in a distributed game-theoretic framework for safety-critical team coordination.
