A skill-aligned fairness framework for multi-agent reinforcement learning, with a composite fairness metric balancing workload equality and skill-task alignment. FairSkillMARL achieves 40-60% higher skill-task alignment than workload-only baselines, with a 70-85% reduction in workload-range disparity while maintaining comparable success rates (83% vs. 80-86%). We also introduce MARLHospital, a MARL benchmark supporting heterogeneous agents with skill heterogeneity, energy levels, and customizable team compositions.
